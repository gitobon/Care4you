Returns & Refund Policy

If you’re not completely satisfied, here’s how to return your order:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery via our Contact page.
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return.
  3. We’ll issue a prepaid return label (if applicable) or direct you to the nearest drop-off point.
  4. Once we receive and inspect your return, we’ll issue a refund to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Perishable items must be returned in their original condition. For questions, see Contact Information.
